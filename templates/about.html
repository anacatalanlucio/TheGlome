Nice to meet you!

I am a Computer Science student. I currently live in Montreal, Canada. I was born in Mexico City. My parents are both Mexican. We moved to Montreal when I was 7 years old. I've lived in Montreal most of my life but I had the opportunity to live abroad a few times as I was growing up.

What is THEGLOME?

THEGLOME is a simple blog that includes topics related to mental health, the environment, well-being, technology, books and movies recommendations, and life advice. It is a project that aims to encourage women and girls around the world to reach their full potential without fear or guilt.

Why creating THEGLOME?

I could not find a blog for women that does not revolve around hair, makeup, lifestyle and clothing. So, I decided to create one because I believe that is is as important to take care of the inside as much as we take care of the outside.

As Eckart Tolle says in his book A New Earth:

"If you get the inside right, the outside will fall into place."

I was not sure if this blog should be in Spanish, English or French. I did not want to limit the amount of people that could be interested in THEGLOME's content, that is why the blog is in English. For I am looking forward to translate the blog in all three languages eventually.
